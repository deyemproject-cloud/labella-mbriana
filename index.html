The Legend of the House

In Neapolitan folklore, La Bella 'Mbriana is the benevolent spirit of the house. We invoke her warmth and hospitality in every dish we serve.

Born from a desire to bring true Neapolitan trattoria culture to the modern table, our kitchen respects the slow rhythms of tradition. From the 24-hour leavened pizza dough to the slow-cooked Ragù Napoletano, everything is made with patience and love.
